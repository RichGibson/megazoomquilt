---

## Summary

These txt files are hand-maintained shooting logs from 2007–2008. They record:
- Source image ranges (e.g., `IMG_6974.JPG` to `IMG_7047.JPG`) for each panorama
- The volume/directory those images lived on
- Gigapan.com IDs (when uploaded)
- Stitch status notes (often raw and expressive)
- GPS waypoint names used as short identifiers

**This is the only surviving record of which source images belong to which panorama for early panos.**

## Cross-Reference with gigapan_list.json

The source logs give **exact image ranges** for IDs in gigapan_list.json. This means:
- If source images on a DVD or volume are found, they can be matched to a specific pano
- The wpt naming (k1, l1, mf01, ln1) is the internal label; gigapan.com may have a different title
- Many early IDs (sub-2000) have no source path in these logs — they predate the logging system
